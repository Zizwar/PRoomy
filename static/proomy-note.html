Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 3, 2024

1. Data Collection

Proomy Note does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information to external servers. All your notes and data remain on your device.

2. Local Storage

All data generated within Proomy Note, including your notes, is stored locally on your device. This ensures that your information is under your control and not accessible to third parties.

3. Open Source

As an open-source application, the code for Proomy Note is publicly available for review, modification, and distribution. You can access the source code at GitHub Repository.

4. Third-Party Services

Proomy Note does not use any third-party services that could collect or process your data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, so please review it periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or by opening an issue.
